## 一、1992年HBR訪談 — 最早公開的戰略思想

**來源**：Harvard Business Review, January 1992, "Japanese-Style Entrepreneurship"
**背景**：SoftBank成立11年，首次在主流英語商業媒體發聲，Son 34歲。

### 框架化選擇業務
[一手] "I came up with 40 new business ideas and used about 25 success measures to evaluate them, including wanting to fall in love with a particular business for the next 50 years at least."
[推斷] 1992年的Son已展示極端理性主義框架——40個選項、25個評分標準——但核心篩選器是感性的「戀愛50年」。這組合奠定他的決策DNA：數量化+直覺終審。

### 軟件即智慧
[一手] "Hardware is the bone...software is the wisdom. And data is the knowledge. Wisdom and knowledge represent the most valuable assets."
[推斷] 1992年他已提出這個三分法，比「數據即石油」的流行說法早了二十年。

### 白手空拳的創業演講
[一手] "I have very little money, very little business experience. I have no product, nothing. What I do have is the greatest enthusiasm, the greatest desire to succeed."（對第一個客戶Joshin Denki的推銷詞）

### 對員工的第一次預言
[一手] "In five years, I'm going to have $75 million in sales...we'll be number one in PC software distribution."（對最初兩名員工說，二人旋即辭職，認為老闆瘋了）

## 二、David Rubenstein Show — 一手口述精華

**來源**：David Rubenstein Show，英文電視訪談（年份未標明，推斷2017-2018年，Vision Fund剛啟動後）
**性質**：正式電視訪談，英文，逐字記錄，Son以第一身回答，語言較隨意。

### 從歧視到動力
[一手] "I had some experience. But I feel now it was good. That made me stronger to work harder. So I had to prove that I am not different from any other guys, you know, not inferior. It's the same. So I had to work harder to prove the value."
[一手] "That made me even more harder because I was feeling that I was hiding something. It was even tougher."（論被迫改用日本名）

### 對Vision Fund的說法
[一手] "So, one vision, which is singularity. Singularity is the concept that the computing power, computer's artificial intelligence, surpassed mankind's brains."
[一手]（對MBS的pitch）"I want to give you a massive gift, the Tokyo gift, a trillion dollar gift. You invest $100 billion to my fund. I give you a trillion dollars."
[推斷] 他把Vision Fund的推銷框架為「慷慨饋贈」而非資金募集——體現他把巨大的交易重構為機遇贈予，而非需求。

### 投資Jack Ma的邏輯
[一手] "He had no business plan. And zero revenue. Employees, maybe 35, 40 employees. But his eyes were very strong, strong eyes, strong shining eyes. I could tell from the way he talked, the way he looked at, he has a charisma, he has a leadership."
[推斷] 這是他「眼神測試」（eye sparkle test）最直接的一手描述。所有理性分析均落敗於非言語的直覺信號。

### Berkeley時期：每天五分鐘發明法
[一手] "I said, you know, five minutes I would allow other than study. I have to make money. I want to earn $10,000 per month. And I would allow me, myself, five minutes a day."
[一手] "So I set alarm clock five o'clock, five minutes. And tick, tick, tick. In five minutes, I said, come, invention, come."
[一手] "The first electronic dictionary, the many students use electronic dictionary. The first one ever made was by myself."（電子詞典賣給Sharp，收入$1.7M）

### 承諾對母親
[一手] "She said, no, you don't come back. This is the forever, you know, farewell. No, I promise you, don't worry. I promise you. So I kept my word."（解釋為何從矽谷返日本）

### 對網絡泡沫崩潰
[一手] "One year before that, actually my net worth, personal net worth was increasing $10 billion per week. For three days I became richer than Bill Gates."
[一手] "In six months after that, our share price went down 99%. So we, we all almost went bankrupt. And somehow I survived."

### 50年人生計劃
[一手] "In my age of 19, I created 50 years of my life plan. And in my age of 60s, from between 60 and 69, I would decide my successor and have my successor keep on running it."

### 對機器人/AI的立場
[一手] "When the robot super intelligence go beyond mankind's intelligence, and they say, well, fighting is the not efficient way of living. The harmony is better. It's more social."
[一手] "To protect human from all those, you know, sadness is a good thing."

## 三、SoftBank集團2024年報 CEO致辭

**來源**：SoftBank Group Annual Report 2024, CEO Message（英文官方）
**性質**：書面正式文件，法律責任文字，Son親自起草（代筆後審核）

### ASI的定義與時間表
[一手] "ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence) that is ten thousand times more intelligent than human wisdom — I anticipate its realization within approximately ten years."
[一手] "ASI is not merely equivalent to human genius. It is something categorically different. It will require collaboration across chips, data centers, and robotics, with power generation emerging as potentially the biggest bottleneck."

### ARM的回報
[一手] "Arm's acquisition for ¥3.3 trillion in 2016 has generated ¥24.6 trillion in returns, representing a 10x multiple on invested capital."

### 核心使命的一貫性
[一手] "Despite perceptions of frequent business pivots, our core mission remains constant: Information Revolution — Happiness for everyone."

## 四、SoftBank World 2024主題演講

**來源**：SoftBank World 2024, October 2024，官方講演
**性質**：公開主題演講，Son親自主講

[一手] "I firmly believe that within the next 10 years, we will reach a level of intelligence with ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence) that is 10,000 times greater than human intellect."

[二手，via Rubenstein] 歷年演講以獨特的「預言者」風格著稱：手持巨型圖表、以天文數字作坐標、情緒激動時哽咽——都是他演講的標誌性元素。

## 六、反複出現的核心論點

### 1. 300年計劃 ⭐⭐⭐
[二手，via多家媒體] "Companies must have much longer life cycles than people. I want to build a company that will continue to grow for at least 300 years."
[推斷] 這是他所有策略的框架性前提，不是比喻，他字面上相信它。

### 2. 奇點信仰 ⭐⭐⭐
[一手] 「30年內AI智能將超越人類大多數領域」——貫穿2017至2024年所有公開演講的核心主張。

### 3. 眼神測試 ⭐⭐
[一手] Jack Ma的「strong shining eyes」是$20M投資的根本理由——理性框架之外的人類信號感知。

### 4. Information Revolution — Happiness for everyone ⭐⭐⭐
SoftBank官方使命，Son在所有場合反複引用，跨越30年從未更換。
