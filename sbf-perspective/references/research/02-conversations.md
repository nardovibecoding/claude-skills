## 一、The Block播客 — 崩盤後巴哈馬現場採訪（2022年11月）

**主持人**：Frank Shapiro（The Block編輯）
**場景**：SBF仍在巴哈馬公寓，被追問一切——崩盤原因、Alameda關係、個人責任

### 開場：為何還在巴哈馬
[一手] "I think a lot of people would [want to go home]. I guess from my perspective, it's not about looking to go home or something like that. Or looking to be in an escape hatch or something like that."

[一手] "There still are things that need to get done. There are still a lot of global teams, a lot of global regulators, administrators who are working on and with FTX."

### 論Alameda的特殊帳戶
[一手] "I think [Alameda] may have had something like a delayed liquidation account near the end. I don't, I'm not entirely confident in that fact. I was not Alameda's... really, and this speaks to part of the problem. No one was really Alameda's core."

[一手] "I feel embarrassed to not be confident about whether that is true or not, because that is a thing that I, as CEO, should be confident about."

### 論客戶資金流向
[一手] "FTX did not have its own bank accounts [in 2019-2020]... clients who wanted to wire money to FTX... would wire money to Alameda's bank account. And they would do this because that was the only way that they could wire money and then trade on FTX at the time."

[一手] "My best guess of the answer... I don't feel entirely confident about this answer... this is cobbled together between what I was able to put together in the wake of what happened between vague recollections I had."

### 論CEO責任
[一手] "It gets to, at the very least, a dereliction of my duty... it was my job as CEO to make sure that all of the important jobs were getting done."

[一手] "I don't particularly blame anyone else for this."

### 論知情程度
[一手] "I don't know exactly who knew what when. I don't want to put words in other people's mouths."

[一手] "I'm not entirely sure how those decisions... became made."

[一手] "I honestly am not sure." (on the $55M loan to Ryan Salame)

[一手] "Dollar bills are generally fungible." (attempting to explain Robinhood share purchase financing)

### 論11月7日刪除推文
[一手] "I tweeted it with... an outlook on things that I, within a day or so, began to feel was not realistic."

[一手] "I vetoed a lot of versions of it that I thought were not true... and pared it down quite a bit. But even given that... that tweet didn't really reflect where my head was at very much."

### 論懲罰與責任
**主持人問**："Do you believe you deserve to be punished in some way?"
[一手] "That's not my decision to make."

**追問**："Just yes or no — do you believe you deserve to be punished?"
[一手] "I think about it in terms of having a duty to do what's right. And a duty to do everything that I can make up for."

[一手] "The thing I feel strongly is that they deserve to have a duty to make it right, no matter what it costs them as best they can."

### 論如何現在感受
[一手] "I don't feel good right now... when I reflect on what's happened on the impact that I've had. Like that does not feel good... I don't think there's any... rate particularly for me to feel good about it. It sucks."

## 二、Andrew Ross Sorkin / DealBook峰會（2022年11月）

**來源**：[二手整合，via research.md及各媒體報道]

[一手，via 媒體引述] SBF在Sorkin訪談中聲稱從11月6日開始察覺到問題苗頭，但數天前仍對外聲稱一切正常。

[推斷] Sorkin提問風格比Frank Shapiro更直接，SBF在這場訪談中也確認了Alameda對FTX有「特殊訪問權限」，成為後來各家媒體追問的基礎。

## 三、FTX全盛期採訪（2021-2022年初）— 角色扮演模板

**性質**：SBF在巔峰期展現的公開形象，是角色扮演的核心參考層

[推斷，基於research.md] 全盛期採訪語言特徵：
- 大量使用expected value（EV）、utilitarian calculus框架
- 把一切商業決定包裝為「最大化對世界的正面影響」
- 語速快、充滿統計學語言、表現得像個不在乎金錢只在乎使命的數學天才
- 玩League of Legends接受採訪、穿短褲T恤出席金融論壇——刻意的反建制符號

[二手，via TIME雜誌] EA領袖事先被警告過SBF的行為問題，但在他捐款數千萬給EA事業期間，社群選擇保持沉默。

## 四、Senate Banking Committee聽証（2022年11月）— 被取消的發言

[推斷] SBF原定出席美國參議院銀行委員會聽證，但在逮捕前的混亂期間未能成行。「缺席的聽證」本身成為一個象徵事件。

## 五、Coffeezilla訪談（崩盤後）

[二手，via research.md] Coffeezilla YouTube系列「The Collapse of FTX」被研究資料列為最佳第三方敘述。SBF在其中接受訪問，繼續使用同一套「我誤判但沒有惡意」的敘事框架。

## 六、法庭證詞（2023年10月審判）

[二手，via Wikipedia/Britannica整合]

SBF出庭作供，主要主張：
- 不知道Alameda以FTX客戶資金進行交易
- 不是蓄意詐欺，是誤判和管理失職
- 相信FTX可以渡過危機

[推斷] 陪審團不接受這套說法。7項詐欺及共謀罪名全部成立。
