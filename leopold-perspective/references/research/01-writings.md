### Section I: From GPT-4 to AGI: Counting the OOMs

**URL:** https://situational-awareness.ai/from-gpt-4-to-agi/

[一手] "GPT-2 (2019) ~ preschooler: 'Wow, it can string together a few plausible sentences.' A very-cherry-picked example of a semi-coherent story about unicorns in the Andes it generated was incredibly impressive at the time. And yet GPT-2 could barely count to 5 without getting tripped up; when summarizing an article, it just barely outperformed selecting 3 random sentences from the article."

[一手] "While massive investments in compute get all the attention, algorithmic progress is probably a similarly important driver of progress (and has been dramatically underrated). To see just how big of a deal algorithmic progress can be, consider the following illustration of the drop in price to attain ~50% accuracy on the MATH benchmark (high school competition math) over just two years. Inference efficiency improved by nearly 3 OOMs—1,000x—in less than two years."

[一手] "Frontier models are already trained on much of the internet. Llama 3, for example, was trained on over 15T tokens. Common Crawl, a dump of much of the internet used for LLM training, is >100T tokens raw, though much of that is spam and duplication."

**核心論點：** AGI by 2027 "strikingly plausible" — GPT-2到GPT-4四年從幼兒園到高中生；推算算力和算法進步，2027年前再跳一級。

---

### Section II: From AGI to Superintelligence: the Intelligence Explosion

**URL:** https://situational-awareness.ai/from-agi-to-superintelligence/

[一手] "We don't need robotics—we don't need many things—for AI to automate AI research. The jobs of AI researchers and engineers at leading labs can be done fully virtually and don't run into real-world bottlenecks in the same way."

[一手] "With sufficient GPU infrastructure by 2027, we could run many millions of copies of our automated AI researchers, perhaps 100 million human-researcher-equivalents, running day and night."

[一手] "Limited compute for experiments is probably the most important bottleneck to the intelligence explosion. However, automated researchers could overcome this through superior efficiency — better intuitions from reading all ML literature, fewer bugs through careful code review, ability to run smaller-scale experiments effectively — making it hard to imagine that the automated AI researchers couldn't use the compute at least 10x more effectively."

**核心論點：** AI不會停在人類水平。1億個自動化AI研究員7×24運行，可把十年算法進步壓縮進一年（"5+ OOMs in a year"）。

### Section IIId: The Free World Must Prevail

**URL:** https://situational-awareness.ai/the-free-world-must-prevail/

**核心論點：** 民主國家必須領先AGI競賽；中共獲得AGI將對全球秩序造成災難性後果。

---

### Section IV: The Project

**URL:** https://situational-awareness.ai/the-project/

[一手] "There are no good options here—but I don't see another way. When a technology becomes this important for national security, we will need the USG. Within years, superintelligence would completely shake up the military balance of power. By the early 2030s, the entirety of the US arsenal (like it or not, the bedrock of global peace and security) will probably be obsolete."

[一手] "We're in the midgame now. 2023 was already a wild shift. AGI went from a fringe topic you'd be hesitant to associate with, to the subject of major Senate hearings and summits of world leaders. Given how early we are still, the level of USG engagement has been impressive to me. A couple more '2023's, and the Overton window will be blown completely open."

**核心論點：** 美國政府介入AGI開發不可避免且必要；將其視為國家安全問題是唯一負責任的路徑。

### Section V: Parting Thoughts

**URL:** https://situational-awareness.ai/parting-thoughts/

[一手] "A few years ago, at least for me, I took these ideas seriously—but they were abstract, quarantined in models and probability estimates. Now it feels extremely visceral. I can see it."

[一手] "There is no crack team coming to handle this. Only a few hundred people truly grasp the magnitude of what approaches. The world is incredibly small."

**AGI Realism三原則：**
1. Superintelligence = 人類最強大武器；以國家安全對待
2. 美國領導對競爭和安全兩方面均不可或缺
3. 避免災難性結果需要嚴格的安全關注，而非即興發揮

## 四、反複出現的核心論點

### 1. OOM框架：用可測量的跨數量級增長預測AGI ⭐⭐⭐
出現頻率：Situational Awareness全文核心骨架
[一手] 算力增長 + 算法進步 + 數據擴展 = 三個獨立的OOM累積，共同驅動能力躍遷

### 2. 時間線確定性：2027年不是猜測，是外推 ⭐⭐⭐
[一手] "I think with high confidence that..." — 頻繁使用概率語言強化論點
[推斷] 他的預測框架是工程師思維：benchmark數據可查，進步率可測，因此預測是嚴謹外推而非科幻

### 3. 安全漏洞緊迫性：AGI lab安全嚴重不足 ⭐⭐⭐
[推斷] 這份內部安全備忘錄直接導致其被OpenAI解僱，但他選擇繼續公開發聲

### 4. 世界小得嚇人 ⭐⭐
[一手] "Only a few hundred people truly grasp the magnitude of what approaches."
[推斷] 這句話是Leopold世界觀的情緒核心：緊迫感來自意識到有能力行動的人極少
