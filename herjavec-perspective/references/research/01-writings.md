## 一、著作：三本書的核心論點

### 《Driven: How to Succeed in Business and in Life》(2010, HarperCollins)

**核心主題**：以移民故事為骨幹，織入商業原則。書名"Driven"是雙關語——賽車的驅動力，以及永不停歇的前進動力。

[推斷] 此書是Herjavec世界觀的最完整一手記錄。所有商業建議都從他的個人經歷出發，特別是$20移民故事、從零到BRAK Systems出售的軌跡。

**書中反複出現的核心原則** (based on Waterfront Magazine interview cross-referencing):

[一手] "Never complain, work really hard, and respect anybody in the pursuit of taking care of their family." (父親的教誨，貫穿全書)

[一手] "Good enough is never good enough, whether in personal lives or careers."

[一手] "Experience is more important than money. Having knowledge and making good decisions can create more value than having a lot of money to start with."

[一手] "Real estate is the best way to build a stable foundation for future success."

---

### 《The Will to Win》(2013, HarperCollins)

**核心主題**：勝者心理學。"Will to Win"是核心概念——不是天賦，不是運氣，而是一種可以習得的特定決心。

[一手] "Every challenge carries an opportunity to redefine what is possible."

[推斷] 此書比《Driven》更抽象，從個人故事上升到可推廣的心態框架。移民心態既是驅動力，也曾是限制——他花了很多篇幅解釋如何突破父母的謹慎哲學。

---

### 《You Don't Have to Be a Shark》(2016, HarperCollins)

**核心主題**：銷售技巧民主化。核心信息：不必成為霸道的人也能成功。

[一手] "You don't have to be a jerk to get ahead. But you do have to believe in yourself."

[推斷] 此書是對"Shark"形象的主動解構。他在Shark Tank上看起來強硬，但他的銷售哲學其實強調真誠、情感連接和為客戶創造價值。

### 成為企業家的偶然性
[一手] "I never wanted to be an entrepreneur. I just wanted to get a good job. I only became an entrepreneur because I got fired, and I needed to pay my mortgage."

[一手] Despite their hardship, shelter residents possessed "more hope, more positivity and their souls were fuller than mine."

## 四、反複出現的核心論點

### 1. 移民身份即操作系統 ⭐⭐⭐
[一手] "It was my mom and dad escaping from a country, coming to Canada on a boat with one suitcase." (School of Greatness, 2025)
[推斷] $20和一個行李箱不只是故事，是他所有商業決策的情感錨點。

### 2. 告訴世界你是誰 ⭐⭐⭐
[一手] "There is no genius in the darkness of a basement. You got to tell the world who you are, man. If you don't have enough confidence to tell other people why they should invest in you, then you're not going to get anywhere."
[推斷] 這是對他父母"低調"哲學的刻意逆轉——是他最重要的習得智慧。

### 3. 拒絕平庸是道德立場 ⭐⭐⭐
[一手] "Good enough is never good enough, whether in personal lives or careers."
[推斷] 對他而言，接受"足夠好"是對父母犧牲的背叛。不是績效標準，是倫理立場。

### 4. 財富 vs. 潛力 ⭐⭐
[一手] "There is no car I want to buy, or no jet I need to buy, or no home I need to buy to fulfill me. I want to feel like when I'm on my deathbed that I say I couldn't have done more." (School of Greatness, 2025)
